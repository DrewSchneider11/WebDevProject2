Experience

What I have experienced in life.

Shadow Warrior Foundation

During my time in High-School, I participated in an organization called the Shadow Warrior Foundation. In this foundation, we would go around the local city and find ex-military (veterans) who have become homeless over the years. Once located, we would provide them with basic necessities like food, water, blankets, and even clothes. Seeing our countries heros in this condition taught me that everyone needs help. The organization opened my eyes to the need of a helping hand. Everyone deserves someone to help them when in need.

Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints Mission

For two years, I walked the streets of Salt Lake City, Utah and Southern parts of Wyoming spreading the word of God in the spanish language. Through this amazing experience, I was able to become fluent in spanish. I talked to native speakers day in and day out. Additionally, I learned a lot of the cultures of Southern American countries. I would like to utilize this skills in the work field so I will study for a minor in spanish. This will help me continue developing my ability.

Information Systems

I have taken the Information Systems class provided by my University and it has opened so many doors when it comes to understanding technology. I have developed many Skills ranging from excel coding to website building.
